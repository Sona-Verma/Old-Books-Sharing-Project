About Online Book Share


We are a group of book lovers and our endeavor has always been to come up with world class products in the books and publishing space. Our products and services would always enable the book lovers have a pleasant experience across platforms – be it reading or exchanging their ‘well-read’ titles for new ones or writing about what they have read.
This platform http://GyanBhandar.atsnx.com was born out of a genuine need of book lovers to get their ‘well read’ books to another book lover like themselves. The biggest nightmare of a book lover is to see his book being sold as part of a ‘waste-paper pile’!
http://GyanBhandar.atsnx.com we help you not to go through that nightmarish experience.
Visit us at http://GyanBhandar.atsnx.com use the platform for free – give us your treasures and we will make sure it reaches another book lover such as yourself. And choose from thousands of books for yourself to read.... All you pay for is the cost of shipping( a small price to avoid your worst nightmare ).
Its easy, its economical and an opportunity for you to play a small part in protecting the environment by recycling.

                                                                                                                      We hope you have a great experience on all our platforms.should you have any questions or feedback.
