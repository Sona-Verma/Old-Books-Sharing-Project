READERS

1.What is the Gyanbhandaar?

Gyanbhandaar is an online book exchange platform, an initiative to help book lovers give their ‘well read’ books to a fellow book lover and get different books from the community, in exchange.Bookxchange.in offers secure online exchange of books through the website. You can exchange your used books and pick from thousands of titles across genres in exchange.

2.Is it free?

Registration on the platform and the exchange of books is FREE.

3.How can I exchange the books online?

Log in to gyanbhandaar and enter details enter details of the books you'd like to give and submit. Then browse the bookshelf and pick the books you'd like to have, and click on SUBMIT.

4.Can students order textbooks?

We plan to make textbooks for medical, engineering, and science available on Gyanbhandaar in the near future.

5.Do I need any special registration?

No. The registration process is the same for every member that wants to access, submit and exchange books on the platform.
